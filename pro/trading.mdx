Pro Mode uses the same trading interface as Rookie Mode with several important additions designed for real-capital trading.

## What's different from Rookie

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No capital constraint bar">
    Instead of the $100 daily capital indicator, Pro Mode shows your full portfolio balance and all-time P&L in the top right of the trading interface.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Piker Score impact indicator">
    A quiet line below the leverage slider shows when your current order size is likely to affect your Sharpe ratio or drawdown score: *"Large position may affect your Sharpe."* This only appears when leverage is above 10x.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Leaderboard mini widget">
    A collapsible strip above the order book shows your current rank vs the #1 trader. Always one glance away from knowing where you stand.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Funding rate prominence">
    Funding rate is displayed prominently in the price ticker. In Pro Mode it directly affects your real P&L and your Sharpe ratio score.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Liquidation price in red">
    When a position approaches its liquidation price, the liquidation figure in the positions tab turns red. A notification is also triggered.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Same pairs, same leverage tiers

Pro Mode supports the same 20 pairs with the same leverage caps as Rookie Mode. The caps exist for the same reason — to keep the simulation realistic and the scoring system meaningful.

## Trading with backed capital

If you have Fund Me enabled and backers have deployed capital, that capital appears in your portfolio alongside your own. You trade it the same way — the profit split happens automatically at the end of each week via the Solana smart contract.
