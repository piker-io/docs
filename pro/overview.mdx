Pro Mode is where serious traders compete. There are no daily resets, no virtual capital limits, and no artificial time pressure. Your performance accumulates continuously into a Piker Score that determines your tier, your reputation, and your access to real community capital.

## What's different from Rookie

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="No reset" icon="infinity">
    Pro Mode never resets. Your portfolio, your score, and your rank evolve continuously. There are no seasons or weekly cycles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Piker Score" icon="chart-line">
    Ranked by a composite score across five dimensions — not just raw P&L. Rewards consistent, risk-managed trading over lucky big wins.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real capital" icon="dollar-sign">
    Trade with your own capital or with community-backed USDC through Vaults. Real money, real stakes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tier system" icon="layer-group">
    Five tiers from Bronze to Elite. Each tier unlocks new features, profile frames, and eventually vault access.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting to Pro Mode

Pro Mode is accessible to any user at any time. There is no evaluation or gate to pass. However, opening a Vault — where backers deploy real USDC to support you — requires meeting eligibility criteria based on your Rookie Mode track record.

<Tip>
  Building a strong Rookie Mode history before going Pro gives you a head start. Backers can see your Rookie stats and use them to evaluate whether to back you.
</Tip>

## The gold world

Pro Mode has its own visual identity — gold and warm purple, versus Rookie Mode's silver and cool purple. Every screenshot from Pro Mode shows a gold **PRO** watermark on the chart. Your profile frame upgrades based on your tier. The distinction is intentional: anyone who sees your Pro screenshot knows they're looking at real stakes.
