---
title: Tiers
description: Bronze through Elite — what each tier means and unlocks
---

Your tier is determined by your Piker Score relative to all other Pro Mode traders. Tiers update in real time — if your score rises enough, you upgrade immediately. If it drops, you can downgrade.

## Tier breakdown

| Tier | Piker Score Range | % of Traders | What it unlocks |
|------|------------------|--------------|----------------|
| Bronze | 0 – 25th percentile | ~25% | Basic profile frame, access to Pro leaderboard |
| Silver | 25th – 50th percentile | ~20% | Silver profile frame, double border |
| Gold | 50th – 75th percentile | ~15% | Gold glow effect on profile |
| Diamond | 75th – 95th percentile | ~8% | Prismatic profile frame, expanded Fund Me cap |
| Elite | Top 5% | ~2% | Animated Elite frame, Vault access eligibility |

<Warning>
  Tiers are relative — they're based on your percentile among all active Pro traders, not an absolute score threshold. As the platform grows and competition increases, maintaining a tier requires continued performance.
</Warning>

## Tier milestone moments

When you cross into a new tier, Piker shows a full-screen milestone reveal — the tier name, your new frame, and what you've unlocked. This is a shareable moment. The share card generated at a tier upgrade shows your new tier badge, Piker Score, and a gold PRO watermark.

## Elite tier and Vault access

Elite is the top 5% of all Pro traders. Reaching Elite unlocks eligibility for Vault access — the ability to trade with community-backed USDC through Fund Me, with the platform's full backing and visibility.

<Info>
  Tier drops can happen. If your score falls due to poor recent performance, your tier adjusts accordingly. Vault access is suspended if you drop below Elite tier.
</Info>
