Milestones are the memorable events of Pro Mode — the moments worth screenshotting. Every milestone generates a share card with a gold **PRO** watermark and drops a permanent marker on your profile.

## Types of milestones

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Tier upgrades" icon="arrow-up">
    Crossing from one tier to the next — Bronze to Silver, Silver to Gold, all the way to Elite. Each upgrade triggers a full-screen reveal and a new profile frame.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Score thresholds" icon="gauge-high">
    Hitting a Piker Score of 500, 750, 900, or 1000 for the first time. Each threshold is a permanent badge on your profile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fund Me milestones" icon="handshake">
    First backer, $1k backed, $10k backed, first week of positive returns for your backers. These build your funded-trader reputation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance records" icon="medal">
    Personal bests — highest single-week ROI, longest positive equity streak, deepest drawdown recovery. Private records that become public when broken.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Pro badges

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🥉 First Tier">
    **Trigger:** Reach Silver tier for the first time.

    Your first step out of Bronze. Silver means your Piker Score is above the 25th percentile of all Pro traders — a real signal that you're competing, not just participating.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🏆 Gold Standard">
    **Trigger:** Reach Gold tier.

    Top 50% of Pro traders. This is where consistent, risk-managed trading starts showing up in your score.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="💠 Diamond Tier">
    **Trigger:** Reach Diamond tier.

    Top 25% of all Pro traders. Expanded Fund Me cap. Backers actively search for Diamond-tier profiles.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="👑 Elite">
    **Trigger:** Reach Elite tier — top 5% of all Pro traders.

    The highest tier on Piker. Unlocks Vault access eligibility and the animated Elite profile frame. Fewer than 1 in 20 Pro traders will ever hold this badge.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🛡️ Untouchable">
    **Trigger:** Maintain a Piker Score above 800 for 30 consecutive days without dropping below.

    Consistency at the top. Harder than reaching a high score — you have to defend it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🌊 Smooth Operator">
    **Trigger:** Reach a Consistency Score of 90+ at any tier.

    The equity curve badge. Awarded for an exceptionally smooth track record — few drawdowns, steady compounding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🤝 Trusted">
    **Trigger:** Deliver positive returns to your backers for 4 consecutive weeks.

    The Fund Me trust badge. Signals to new backers that you've repeatedly turned their capital into profit.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Share cards

Every milestone generates a share card ready for Twitter, Discord, or anywhere else. The card always includes your username, the milestone detail, your Piker Score, and the gold PRO watermark. Tap the share icon from any milestone screen to copy the image or post directly.

<Info>
  Milestone share cards use your current tier frame as the border, so a Gold-tier tier-upgrade card looks different from an Elite-tier one. The visual hierarchy is part of the status.
</Info>
