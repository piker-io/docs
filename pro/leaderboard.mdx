The Pro leaderboard is the permanent ranking of every Pro Mode trader on Piker. Unlike the Rookie board, it never resets — your score and rank carry forward continuously as you trade.

## How ranking works

Pro rank is determined by your Piker Score — the composite metric across ROI, Sharpe, drawdown, consistency, and activity. The highest score sits at #1. Rank updates in real time as trades close and scores recalculate.

See the [Piker Score](/pro/piker-score) page for the full weighting breakdown.

## Filters and views

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Global">
    The full Pro leaderboard, ranked by Piker Score. Shows rank, username, tier badge, Piker Score, all-time ROI, and a Vault indicator if the trader is accepting backers.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="By tier">
    Filter down to a single tier — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, or Elite. Useful when you want to see where you stand against traders at your level, not the entire field.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Vaults only">
    Shows only traders currently accepting backers. This is the discovery feed for anyone looking to deploy capital. Filter further by tier, ROI, or Sharpe ratio.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Following">
    Traders you follow, ranked among themselves. Keep tabs on the specific accounts you care about without the noise of the full board.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  Your own rank is always pinned to the top of the screen when browsing the Pro leaderboard, regardless of which view you're in. You never have to scroll to find yourself.
</Info>

## Weekly movers

A secondary leaderboard highlights traders whose Piker Score has moved the most in the past 7 days — both climbers and fallers. This is where you spot traders on a hot streak before they reach the top of the global board.

<Tip>
  The weekly movers board is the best place to find up-and-coming Vault candidates. Traders trending up often have capacity for new backers before their cap fills.
</Tip>

## Tapping into a trader

Tap any row on the leaderboard to open the trader's public profile. From there you can follow them, review their full Pro and Rookie stats, inspect their trade history, and — if they have a Vault open — back them directly.
