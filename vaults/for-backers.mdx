## Finding traders to back

Traders with a Vault open appear with a green dot on the Pro leaderboard. You can also discover them via:

- **Leaderboard** — filter the Pro Rankings for traders with a Vault open
- **Discover page** — suggested traders section highlights Vault-eligible performers
- **Direct profile** — any trader's public profile shows their Vault card if open

## Evaluating a trader

Before backing anyone, review their full stats on their public profile:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Rookie stats">
    Streak history, best daily P&L, profitable days percentage, all-time rank. These show how the trader performs under the same conditions as everyone else — equal $100 capital, daily competition.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pro stats">
    Piker Score, tier, all-time ROI, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, consistency score. These show how they perform with real capital and real stakes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Trade history">
    If the trader has made their trade history public, you can see every past trade — pair, direction, entry, P&L. No hidden record.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="On-chain verification">
    The trader's rank SBT and badge NFTs are verifiable on Solana. Tap "Verify on Solscan" to confirm the on-chain record matches the profile.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Deploying capital

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your amount">
    Enter any amount between $10 USDC and the trader's backing cap. Quick-select buttons for common amounts ($100, $250, $500, $1000) are available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the split">
    Confirm the 80/15/5 profit split. Your 15% share of any profits is calculated in real time on your backer dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm on Solana">
    Your USDC is deployed via a Solana smart contract. The transaction is on-chain and transparent. The trader cannot access your principal — only the profits generated by trading it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your backer dashboard

Once deployed, your backer dashboard shows the trader's live Pro Mode P&L, your share of current profits, weekly earnings, and your total backed capital value. Withdrawal is available at the end of each week.

<Warning>
  Past performance does not guarantee future results. Piker displays all available trader stats transparently, but trading involves risk. Only back amounts you are comfortable losing entirely.
</Warning>
