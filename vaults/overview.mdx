Vaults is the feature that connects skilled traders with community capital. If you're consistently profitable in Rookie Mode and active in Pro Mode, you can open a Vault — community members deploy real USDC to your trading, and you keep 80% of everything you make with it.

## How it works

```text
Trader opens a Vault on their profile
              ↓
Backers discover them on the leaderboard
              ↓
Backer deploys USDC via Solana smart contract
              ↓
Trader trades in Pro Mode with that capital
              ↓
Profits split automatically:
     80% → Trader
     15% → Backer
      5% → Platform
```
