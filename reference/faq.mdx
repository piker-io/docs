title: FAQ
description: Common questions answered
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Piker free?">
    Yes. Rookie Mode is completely free and always will be. You get $100 of virtual capital every day at no cost. Pro Mode is also free to access — you only deploy real capital if you choose to use Vaults.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a crypto wallet to sign up?">
    No. You can sign up with email or Google. A Solana wallet is only required if you want on-chain features — badge NFTs sent to your wallet, Vaults as a backer, or verified rank SBTs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I lose all my $100 in Rookie Mode?">
    Nothing. Your 100 USDC reloads at 23:59 UTC. There is no penalty for losing, no lock-out, and no cost. Come back tomorrow with a fresh 100 USDC.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple accounts?">
    No. Multiple accounts are against Piker's terms of service and are actively detected. Multi-account activity results in a permanent ban. Your username is your single permanent identity on the platform.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When does the Rookie leaderboard reset?">
    Daily at 23:59 UTC. The Today leaderboard resets completely. Your All-Time stats and streak history never reset.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is the Piker Score calculated?">
    The Piker Score is a weighted composite: ROI (30%), Sharpe ratio (25%), maximum drawdown (20%), consistency score (15%), and activity (10%). Full details in the [Piker Score](/pro/piker-score) page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What are Vaults and how do I qualify?">
    Vaults let skilled traders attract real USDC from community backers. You keep 80% of profits. Eligibility requires 14+ days of Rookie trading, top 20% all-time Rookie ranking, and 60%+ profitable days. Full details in the [Vaults](/vaults/overview) section.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are my trades on a real exchange?">
    No. All trades are simulated against real market prices — prices are live from Binance, Bybit, and OKX, but no orders are placed on any real exchange. In Rookie Mode, capital is entirely virtual. In Pro Mode via Vaults, capital is real USDC held in a Solana smart contract, but trading activity is still simulated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to my streak if I miss a day?">
    Your streak resets to zero. Any day with no trades placed counts as a missed day. The streak calendar on your profile will show a grey dot for that day.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a mobile app?">
    Piker is a mobile-first web application — it's designed to feel like a native app in your mobile browser. Native iOS and Android apps are on the roadmap.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does the ROOKIE or PRO watermark on screenshots mean?">
    Every chart in Piker shows a faint watermark identifying which mode you're in. This makes screenshots instantly identifiable — anyone who sees your result knows whether it's Rookie (practice) or Pro (real stakes). It's a credibility feature, not a restriction.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is Piker different from a prop firm?">
    Prop firms charge 50–500 USD for the right to attempt a funded evaluation. 90–95% of attempts fail. Piker is free, always. No evaluation fees, no re-attempts, no pay-to-fail loop. Your track record on Piker is public, verifiable on-chain, and can attract community funding based purely on skill.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
