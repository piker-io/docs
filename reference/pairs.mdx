Piker V0 launches with 20 crypto perpetual futures pairs. Leverage caps are set by tier based on asset volatility.

## Pair tiers

| Tier | Pairs | Max Leverage | Maintenance Margin |
|------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Tier 1 — Majors** | BTC/USDT, ETH/USDT | 20x | 0.5% |
| **Tier 2 — Large Caps** | SOL/USDT, BNB/USDT, XRP/USDT, DOGE/USDT, ADA/USDT, AVAX/USDT | 15x | 1.0% |
| **Tier 3 — Mid Caps** | LINK/USDT, MATIC/USDT, DOT/USDT, UNI/USDT, ARB/USDT, OP/USDT | 10x | 1.0% |
| **Tier 4 — Volatility** | PEPE/USDT, WIF/USDT, BONK/USDT, SUI/USDT, SEI/USDT, TIA/USDT | 5x | 1.0% |

## Price feed architecture

All prices are sourced in real time from three exchanges and aggregated into a single composite price:

- Binance WebSocket
- Bybit WebSocket
- OKX WebSocket

The **median price** across all three feeds is used for all order executions. This prevents any single exchange's price anomaly from affecting the simulation and ensures fair execution for all traders.

Price updates are pushed to all connected clients with sub-200ms latency. All price snapshots are logged with timestamps for audit and dispute resolution.

<Info>
  Slippage is simulated for large orders based on real order book depth. A position that would move the market on a real exchange will experience proportional simulated slippage on Piker.
</Info>
