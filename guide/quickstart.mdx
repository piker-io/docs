<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [piker.io](https://piker.io) and sign up with email, Google, or a Solana wallet (Phantom, Backpack, or Solflare). No KYC. No phone number. No verification delays.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your username">
    Pick your username carefully — it becomes your permanent identity on Piker. Your public profile lives at `piker.io/@username` and cannot be changed later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your starting mode">
    You'll see both modes on your first visit. Start in Rookie Mode if you want to practice risk-free with $100 daily virtual capital. Jump straight to Pro Mode if you're ready for real stakes.

    <Tip>
      Most users start in Rookie Mode. It's the best way to understand how the scoring system works before putting real capital at risk.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place your first trade">
    In Rookie Mode, your $100 of virtual capital is ready immediately. Pick a pair from the trading arena, set your size and leverage, and place your first trade. The full order book and chart are available from your very first session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch your leaderboard rank">
    Every trade you make feeds into the daily Rookie leaderboard. After your first profitable trade, your name appears in the global rankings. From here, the daily competition begins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can trade

Piker supports 20 crypto perpetual futures pairs across four tiers:

| Tier | Pairs | Max Leverage |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Majors | BTC/USDT, ETH/USDT | 20x |
| Large Caps | SOL, BNB, XRP, DOGE, ADA, AVAX | 15x |
| Mid Caps | LINK, MATIC, DOT, UNI, ARB, OP | 10x |
| Volatility | PEPE, WIF, BONK, SUI, SEI, TIA | 5x |

<Warning>
  Leverage caps are enforced per tier. Higher volatility assets have lower maximum leverage to keep the simulation realistic and competitive.
</Warning>
