---
title: Daily Capital
description: How the $100 daily reset works
---

Every Rookie Mode user receives $100 of virtual USDT every single day. This is not a balance that accumulates — it resets completely at 23:59 UTC regardless of your performance.

## The reset cycle

```text
Day starts (after 23:59 UTC reset)
        ↓
$100.00 loaded — trade freely all day
        ↓
23:59 UTC — all positions auto-close
        ↓
Final P&L recorded to leaderboard and profile
        ↓
$100.00 reloads — new day begins
```

## Why the same $100 for everyone

The equal capital rule is what makes the Rookie leaderboard fair and meaningful. A trader with $100,000 of real capital has no advantage over a student with nothing. The only variable is skill.

This is the core philosophy of Piker: **the barrier to proving your skill should be zero.**

<Info>
  Your daily P&L percentage matters more than the absolute dollar amount on the Rookie leaderboard. Making $40 on $100 (+40%) ranks higher than making $30 on $100 (+30%).
</Info>

## Capital constraints

| Rule | Value |
|------|-------|
| Daily capital | $100.00 USDT |
| Reset time | 23:59 UTC daily |
| Max concurrent positions | 10 |
| Max position size | 50% of available capital |
| Minimum trade to count | Any — no minimum |

## What happens to undeployed capital

Capital you don't deploy still counts as your base. If you deploy $50 and make $20 profit, your net P&L is +$20 (+20% on $100). Undeployed capital does not earn anything — there is no yield in Rookie Mode.

<Tip>
  The reset timer is always visible in the top right of the Rookie trade view. Watch it when you have open positions near the end of the day.
</Tip>
