---
title: Streaks
description: How daily streaks work and why they matter
---

A streak is the number of consecutive profitable days you've had in Rookie Mode. It resets to zero on any day you end with a net loss or don't trade at all.

## Streak rules

| Day result | Streak effect |
|-----------|--------------|
| Net P&L > $0 (profitable) | Streak increases by 1 |
| Net P&L ≤ $0 (loss or flat) | Streak resets to 0 |
| No trades placed | Streak resets to 0 |

<Warning>
  Streaks reset at 23:59 UTC alongside the capital reset. A profitable open position that gets auto-closed at a loss at 23:59 UTC counts as a loss day.
</Warning>

## Why streaks matter

Streaks appear on your public profile and next to your name on the leaderboard. They signal consistent skill — a 30-day streak tells backers and other traders something a single good day can't.

Streaks also unlock some of the most prestigious Rookie badges. The Voltage badge (7-day streak) and Diamond Hands badge (30-day streak) are among the hardest to earn.

## Streak calendar

Your last 30 days are visualised as a dot calendar on your profile:

- 🟢 Green dot — profitable day
- 🔴 Red dot — loss day
- ⚫ Grey dot — no trade

This calendar is public and visible to anyone who views your profile.

<Tip>
  Even a small profitable trade counts as a profitable day for streak purposes. On days when you're busy or unsure about the market, a single small win is enough to keep your streak alive.
</Tip>
