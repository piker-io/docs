The Rookie leaderboard resets every day at 23:59 UTC alongside the $100 capital. Every trader starts from zero each morning and fights for the top spot with the same resources.

## How ranking works

Rookie leaderboard rank is determined by **net P&L on the $100 daily capital**. The trader who turns $100 into the most by 23:59 UTC wins the day.

Percentage gain is the metric — not absolute dollars. This keeps things fair across all position sizes and leverage choices.

## Three leaderboard views

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Today">
    Live rankings updating in real time throughout the day. Shows rank, username, streak length, daily P&L, and percentage gain. Resets completely at 23:59 UTC.

    Your row is always pinned at the bottom so you can see your rank without scrolling.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="All-Time">
    Cumulative stats that never reset. Ranked by best single-day P&L, total profitable days, and longest streak. This is your permanent Rookie record.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="My Streak">
    Your personal 30-day calendar showing green (profitable), red (loss), and grey (no trade) days. Streak stats: current streak, longest streak, profitable days count, total days traded.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  The Today leaderboard resets daily but your All-Time stats are permanent. Strong all-time stats appear on your public profile and are visible to potential backers in Pro Mode.
</Info>

## Finding other traders

Tap any row on the leaderboard to visit that trader's public Rookie profile. You can follow them to see their trades in your social feed, or — if they have a Vault open — back them with real capital in Pro Mode.
