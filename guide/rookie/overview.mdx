Rookie Mode is the daily competition layer of Piker. Every user — regardless of experience or account size — gets the same $100 of virtual capital every day and competes on the same leaderboard.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive your daily $100">
    At the start of each day (after 23:59 UTC reset), your virtual capital is loaded at $100.00 USDT. This is your entire trading capital for the day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade freely">
    Open and close positions on any of the 20 supported pairs. Use leverage up to the tier cap. Set limit orders, stop losses, and take profits. The full trading interface is available — same chart, same order book, same tools as Pro Mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your P&L">
    Every profitable trade grows your daily P&L. Every losing trade shrinks it. Your net P&L on the $100 is your daily score and determines your position on the leaderboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset at 23:59 UTC">
    At 23:59 UTC, all open positions are automatically closed and evaluated. Your final P&L is recorded. Your $100 reloads. The leaderboard resets. Tomorrow is a fresh start.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Any positions still open at 23:59 UTC are auto-closed at the current market price. Set take-profit and stop-loss orders to avoid unexpected closes. You'll receive a notification 30 minutes before the reset.
</Warning>
