title: Rookie Badges

---

Badges are permanent achievements that appear on your public profile. Once earned, they never disappear. They're a record of specific moments in your trading history.

## Earning badges

Badges unlock automatically when you hit the trigger condition. A notification appears immediately, and the badge is added to your profile. Some Pro badges are also minted as compressed NFTs on Solana.

## All Rookie badges

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🩸 First Blood">
    **Trigger:** First ever profitable day in Rookie Mode.

    The most common badge, but still meaningful. Everyone remembers their first green day. This one stays on your profile forever.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🔥 On Fire">
    **Trigger:** 3-day consecutive profit streak.

    Three green days in a row. Harder than it sounds when markets are volatile.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="⚡ Voltage">
    **Trigger:** 7-day consecutive profit streak.

    A full week of profitable trading. This badge starts to carry real signal about your consistency.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="💎 Diamond Hands">
    **Trigger:** 30-day consecutive profit streak.

    One of the hardest badges to earn. A full month of daily green sessions requires discipline, adaptability, and a lot of good decisions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🎯 Sniper">
    **Trigger:** Finish in the top 10 on the Rookie leaderboard on any single day.

    Top 10 out of all active traders that day. The competition is real.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="👑 Day King">
    **Trigger:** Finish #1 on the Rookie leaderboard for a day.

    Only one trader earns this each day. The hardest daily badge to earn.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="💀 No Mercy">
    **Trigger:** Double your $100 in a single day — reach +$100 P&L or more (+100%).

    Exceptional performance. Requires both skill and calculated risk-taking in the right market conditions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🧊 Ice Veins">
    **Trigger:** Recover from -$50 drawdown to finish the day profitable.

    Down 50% on the day and came back. Psychological resilience and strong execution.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🐉 Resilient">
    **Trigger:** Record a profitable day after 7 consecutive loss days.

    Came back after a brutal week. One of the most respected badges on the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  Badges appear in two states on your profile — full color when earned, greyed out when not yet earned. Tapping an unearned badge shows exactly what you need to do to unlock it.
</Info>
