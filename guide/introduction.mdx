Piker is a free-to-play competitive trading platform. You trade crypto with virtual capital, climb a real leaderboard, build a verifiable public reputation, and if you're good enough, earn the right to trade with real community-backed money.

No evaluation fees. No pay-to-fail loops. Just skill.

## The problem we're solving

The crypto prop trading industry generates USD 12–20 billion annually by charging aspiring traders USD 50–500 per evaluation attempt. 90–95% of participants fail. They pay again. It's a loop designed to extract money from people who actually want to learn.

Meanwhile, free trading simulators are ugly, uninspiring, and have no competitive dimension that would make anyone stay or share.

Piker removes the financial barrier entirely. Compete on skill. Build a reputation. Earn real capital.

## The long term vision

Crypto is just the start. Piker will become the platform to play every financial market, predictions, sports, macro, in one place, with one reputation and one leaderboard.
