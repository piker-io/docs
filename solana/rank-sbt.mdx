Your Pro Mode tier is not just a label on your profile — it's a Soulbound Token (SBT) minted to your Solana wallet. This makes your rank independently verifiable by anyone, anywhere, without having to trust Piker's database.

## What is a Soulbound Token

A Soulbound Token is a non-transferable NFT. It lives in your wallet but cannot be sold, swapped, or sent to another address. This property is critical for rank — a tier you earned must represent your performance, not something you bought from someone else.

<Info>
  SBT ownership is cryptographically tied to the wallet you connected when your tier was assigned. Moving to a new wallet means re-linking your Piker account and re-minting your current tier.
</Info>

## When your SBT is minted

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reach a new tier">
    Your Piker Score crosses into a new tier threshold — Bronze to Silver, Silver to Gold, and so on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic mint">
    Piker's smart contract mints a new SBT representing your current tier and burns the previous one. No action required from you — it happens in the background.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wallet receives it">
    The new SBT appears in your Phantom, Backpack, or Solflare wallet. The metadata records your tier, your Piker Score at mint, and the timestamp.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profile updates">
    Your public Piker profile reflects the new tier frame and adds a "Verified on Solana" link that points directly to the mint transaction on Solscan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the SBT contains

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Tier | Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, or Elite |
| Piker Score | Your score at the time of mint |
| Username | Your Piker username |
| Mint timestamp | Block time of the mint transaction |
| Previous tier | The tier this SBT replaced, if any |

## Tier drops and re-minting

If your Piker Score drops enough to move you into a lower tier, the old SBT is burned and a new one at the lower tier is minted. This keeps your wallet's token always in sync with your live rank.

<Warning>
  Because tier changes trigger on-chain mints, your full tier history is reconstructable from Solscan. Backers can see every upgrade and downgrade, not just your current standing.
</Warning>

## Verifying an SBT

Anyone can verify a trader's rank by:

1. Opening the trader's Piker profile
2. Tapping the "Verified on Solana" link next to their tier badge
3. Inspecting the SBT metadata on Solscan

If the on-chain record doesn't match the profile, the profile is not legitimate.
