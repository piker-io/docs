Piker uses Solana to make your trading reputation permanently verifiable. Your rank, your badges, and your funded vaults are not just numbers in a database — they're on-chain records that anyone can verify independently.

## Why Solana

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Speed" icon="bolt">
    Sub-second transaction finality. Badge mints, rank updates, and vault distributions happen in real time without delays or high fees.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cost" icon="tag">
    Compressed NFTs (cNFTs) on Solana cost fractions of a cent to mint. Issuing thousands of badge NFTs is economically viable at scale.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transparency" icon="eye">
    Every on-chain record is publicly verifiable on Solscan. No Piker employee can alter your rank history, badge record, or vault balance.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What's on-chain

| Item | On-chain format | When it's created |
|------|----------------|------------------|
| Rank SBT | Non-transferable token | When your Pro tier is assigned or updated |
| Rookie badges | Compressed NFT (cNFT) | When you earn a qualifying Rookie badge |
| Pro badges | Compressed NFT (cNFT) | When you earn a Pro badge |
| Vaults | Smart contract | When a backer deploys capital |

## Supported wallets

- Phantom
- Backpack
- Solflare

You can use Piker without a Solana wallet (email and Google sign-up work fine), but connecting a wallet enables on-chain features — badge NFTs go to your wallet, rank SBTs are verifiable, and Vaults require a wallet for USDC transactions.
