title: Badge NFTs
description: Your achievements, minted as compressed NFTs on Solana
---

Every qualifying badge you earn on Piker — Rookie and Pro — is minted as a compressed NFT (cNFT) on Solana. The NFT is yours. It lives in your wallet. It's permanent.

## Why compressed NFTs

Compressed NFTs use Solana's state compression to bring mint costs down to fractions of a cent. This is what makes issuing thousands of badges across the platform economically viable — a traditional NFT mint would be prohibitively expensive at scale.

<Info>
  cNFTs are fully compatible with standard Solana NFT tooling. You can view them in Phantom, Backpack, and Solflare, list them on marketplaces (though Piker badges are non-transferable by design), and verify them on Solscan.
</Info>

## How minting works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Earn the badge">
    You hit the trigger condition for a badge — a 7-day streak, a top-10 daily finish, a Diamond tier promotion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant mint">
    Piker's Solana program mints the cNFT to your connected wallet in the same transaction that awards the badge. You don't sign anything — gas is paid by Piker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Appears on profile and wallet">
    The badge appears in full color on your public profile within seconds. The cNFT shows up in your wallet's NFT tab on its next refresh.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What each badge NFT contains

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Badge name | The display name, e.g. "Diamond Hands" |
| Badge tier | Rookie or Pro |
| Trigger event | The specific action that earned the badge |
| Earned at | Block time of the mint |
| Piker username | Your username at the time of mint |
| Image | The badge artwork |

## Which badges are on-chain

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="All Pro badges">
    Every Pro Mode badge — tier promotions, Piker Score milestones, Vault achievements — is minted as a cNFT. See the [Milestones page](/pro/milestones) for the full list.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Rare Rookie badges">
    The hardest Rookie badges — Voltage (7-day streak), Diamond Hands (30-day streak), Day King (daily #1 finish), No Mercy (100%+ daily P&L), and Resilient (comeback after 7 loss days) — are minted on-chain.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Common Rookie badges">
    Everyday Rookie badges like First Blood and On Fire appear on your profile but are not minted on-chain. The bar for a permanent on-chain record is intentionally high — it makes the minted ones more meaningful.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Verifying a badge

Every on-chain badge has a "Verify" link next to it on the owner's profile. Tapping it opens Solscan with the cNFT's mint address. The metadata, owner, and mint timestamp are all public record.

<Tip>
  If you care about on-chain provenance for your achievements, connect your Solana wallet before you start trading. Badges earned before wallet connection are recorded on your profile but cannot be retroactively minted.
</Tip>
