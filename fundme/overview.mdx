Fund Me is the feature that connects skilled traders with community capital. If you're consistently profitable in Rookie Mode and active in Pro Mode, you can open yourself up to funding — community members deploy real USDC to your trading, and you keep 80% of everything you make with it.

## How it works

```text
Trader enables Fund Me on their profile
              ↓
Backers discover them on the leaderboard
              ↓
Backer deploys USDC via Solana smart contract
              ↓
Trader trades in Pro Mode with that capital
              ↓
Profits split automatically:
     80% → Trader
     15% → Backer
      5% → Platform
```
