title: Profit Split
description: How the 80 / 15 / 5 split works
---

Every profit generated by a Fund Me trader is split automatically by the Solana smart contract at the end of each week.

## The split

| Recipient | Share | Why |
|-----------|-------|-----|
| Trader | **80%** | The majority goes to the person doing the work — researching, executing, managing risk |
| Backer | **15%** | Return on the capital deployed and the trust extended |
| Platform | **5%** | Piker's fee for providing the infrastructure, leaderboard, and discovery layer |

## How it's enforced

The split is not a policy — it's code. The Solana smart contract holds backer capital and distributes profits according to the 80/15/5 ratio automatically at the end of each weekly cycle. Neither the trader nor Piker can alter the distribution.

<Info>
  Only profits are split. Backer principal is protected — it cannot be traded away or withdrawn by the trader. Only the trader's profits from trading activity are distributed.
</Info>

## Loss scenarios

If a trader has a losing week, backers do not receive any distribution that week. The backer's principal remains in the smart contract and rolls over to the next week. Backers only lose capital if the trader's losses exceed specific thresholds defined in the smart contract.
